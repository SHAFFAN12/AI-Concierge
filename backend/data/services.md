
# Our Services

## Dermatology
Our dermatology department is open from Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm. We offer a wide range of services, including:
- Acne treatment
- Eczema and psoriasis management
- Skin cancer screenings
- Cosmetic dermatology

## Dental
Our dental clinic provides comprehensive dental care. Our services include:
- Routine check-ups and cleanings
- Fillings and crowns
- Teeth whitening
- Dental implants

## Cardiology
Our cardiology department is dedicated to providing the best heart care. We offer:
- Diagnostic testing
- Preventive care
- Treatment for heart disease
- Cardiac rehabilitation
